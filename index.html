L&T Vedant Residences

Embrace a smart way of urban living with Chennai’s newest residential landmark at Poonamallee Road, Manapakkam, envisioned by L&T Realty. Spread across 7 acres of residential space in the L&T Innovation Campus, the project has excellent connectivity to the major locations across the city. Moreover, civic infrastructure like schools, hospitals, hotels, shopping malls, commercial hubs, metro stations, and the airport – are all within a 10 km radius. Discover contemporary homes that are thoughtfully designed to grant you the most comfortable living experience.

  • Vastu-compliant 2, 3 & 4 BHK homes.
  • 60% of open space and no common walls.
  • 7 acres of residential land and owned by L&T
  • 25,000 sq.ft. clubhouse and 30+ indoor & outdoor amenities.
